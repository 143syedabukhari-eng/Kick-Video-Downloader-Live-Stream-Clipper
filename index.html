Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Kick Video Downloader & Live Stream Clipper extension handles your information.

1. Absolute Privacy Commitment

We believe your data belongs to you. This extension does not collect, store, track, transmit, or sell any personal data, browsing history, or user identity information. There are no external analytics, tracking scripts, or remote databases involved.

2. Local Operation & Permissions

All operations performed by this extension happen locally on your device. We request the following permissions solely for functional purposes:

  • storage: To synchronize recording status and progress locally across browser tabs.
  • activeTab & host_permissions: To identify and capture video elements on Kick.com when you explicitly click the download or clip button.
  • downloads: To save the finalized video file directly into your device's default downloads folder.

3. Data Security

Since no data is ever transmitted from your device, your information is as secure as your own computer. No media data, stream URLs, or network traffic is ever shared with the developer or any third parties.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page.
